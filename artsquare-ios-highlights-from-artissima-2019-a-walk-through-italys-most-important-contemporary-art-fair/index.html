ArtSquare.io is Making Headlines in the Blockchain World: Our Platform Featured in The Cryptonomist and Bitcoinita

Nov112019

“On one hand, being able to purchase a fraction of a work of art, a historic building, an archaeological site may have more than a few people frown; on the other, it may be the way forward in having people engage in our cultural heritage, which is too often unfairly ignored and neglected.”

Leonardo Brentegani on ArtSquare.io in Bitcoinita.

“The new model of enjoying the art market promoted by the platform is primarily about sharing, smart trading, and anticipating the future. D’Aloia is confident that in “fifty years only fractionalized and listed artworks will be perceived as good assets in an investment portfolio (…).”

Amelia Tomasicchio on ArtSquare.io in The Cryptonomist.

These quotes are pulled out from our latest media features in two widely read Italian-based blogs on the Blockchain and the world of cryptocurrencies. Both the Bitcoinita and The Cryptonomist have celebrated the mission of ArtSquare.io of making the art world accessible through the application of the Blockchain technology.
